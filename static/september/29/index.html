Athenaeum

Kravis Leadership Institute Panel on "Entrepreneurship Across Sectors," a discussion about creativity and entrepreneurship in the world today. Sarah Smith Orr will lead discussion between:

Kara Andrade, who developed an information sharing network on mobile phones in Central America; Marc Brody '83, who created the US-China Environmental Fund; Marc Cooper, a political journalist and founder of Annenberg Digital News; Jack Edwards '66, who finds and works with social entrepreneurs around the world; Sunil Rajaraman '01, CEO of Scripped, a popular screenwriting tool; Rick Wartzman, columnist for BusinessWeek and fellow at the New America Foundation.

Ath opens at 5; dinner begins at 6; lecture (open to all) begins at 6:45.
