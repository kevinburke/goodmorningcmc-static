Events on campus

Adjusting to College, CUC Workshop. Is college not what you expected? 12pm-1pm, Tranquada Student Services (opposite the library)

The Open Door Information Session. For gay, lesbian, transgender, bisexual, straight or questioning individuals. Led by Carrie Park, M.D. 4pm, Tranquada Student Services.

Accounting and Finance Career Fair. Co-hosted by CMC, Becker Professional Education and Keller Graduate School of Management. 6:00-9:00, 930 Wilshire Blvd. Los Angeles, CA.

Financial Economics Sequence Information Session. For students interested in taking the Financial Economics Sequence. 6pm-6:30pm, Founders Room, Bauer North.

On the Loose, First Meeting. On the Loose is the 5C Outdoor Club. At the meeting you'll find other people interested in the outdoors, and they'll plan cool trips. You'll also learn about all the stuff that OTL does. Check out their website. Meeting in Walker Lounge on Pomona's campus (near the bell tower), 9 PM.
