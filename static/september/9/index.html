Athenaeum

David Oliver Relin. Relin coauthored the book "Three Cups of Tea" with Greg Mortenson. After Mortenson's life was saved by villagers in Pakistan, he decided to devote his life to building schools in the distressed areas of Pakistan and Afghanistan. The Ath opens at 5 for discussion, wine and cheese. Dinner is served at 6 p.m. Talk begins at 6:45 p.m. Place reservations here, or check out the full list of speakers for the fall 2010 semester.
