Athenaeum

Lunch: John West, Author of The Last Goodnights: Assisting My Parents with their Suicides (A Memoir).

Ath opens at 11:30; program begins at 12.

Dinner: Hugh Rockoff, economics professor at Rutgers. If you've ever seen the classic movie The Wizard of Oz, there's a theory that the movie's plea to "follow the yellow brick road" is an allegory to US monetary policy, that it should get back onto the gold standard. Rockoff is one of the proponents of this theory. He will be speaking about "The Wizard of Oz as Monetary Allegory."

Ath opens at 5; dinner starts at 6, and the program begins at 6:45.
