Talks

  • Atul Vyas Memorial Lecture. Atul Vyas was a senior at CMC who killed in a Metrolink accident shortly after I arrived at CMC, and every year since the CMC Math Department has conducted a memorial lecture in his name. The speaker is Peter Schroder, from Caltech, and the topic is "Geometric Modeling and Animation with Meshes." Davidson Lecture Hall, 3-4PM
  • Paul Nerenberg, UC Berkeley, "Physics Meets Biology: Understanding Collagen Degradation with Computational Models," Galileo-Edwards Hall (HMC), 4:15pm
  • Rivka Weinberg, Scripps, "Giving Babies to the Needy: A Critique of Altruistic Surrogacy." Hampton Room (above Malott, Scripps), noon
  • Mark Abdollahian and Hal Nelson, "Computational Political Economy: Citing New Energy Infrastructure," Albrecht Auditorium (CGU), 11:45AM
  • Adam Fingerhut, Loyola Marymount, "Sexual minorities and minority and majority group members"
