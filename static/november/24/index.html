How I find all of the cool and interesting stuff I put in the emails

I would strongly recommend adding an RSS feeds to your daily routine. If you check a ton of different websites that update periodically, like a blog or a news site, RSS is a technology that displays all of the updates from every site in one central place, so you can read through a ton of sites quickly (or watch a short video on RSS for beginners). I started reading RSS feeds before I came to college and I think reading my RSS feed every day has been about as useful as my classes in teaching me about the world. A good RSS reader is Google Reader.

For those of you driving home, you might want to download some podcasts to listen to in the car. Three of my favorites are This American Life, Wait, Wait, Don't Tell Me, and Radiolab. You can go through the entire Radiolab archive too. One of my favorite episodes is called Limits.
