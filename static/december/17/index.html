Leaving Campus

If you are in a dorm, you need to check out before you leave. Get a key envelope from your RA, complete the information, place your room key inside and return the envelope to Story House.

You should also defrost your refrigerator, move your bike into your room and bring in all patio furniture from outside.

Returning Textbooks

There are 3 options for returning textbooks:

Amazon Marketplace. By far the deepest market and best prices for your textbook. You will have to ship the book once you sell it however. Amazon has helpful tutorials on their website.

College MatchersThis site was started by CMC students, no word on quality, but you are helping out a campus entrepreneur by listing your book here.

Resell to Huntley - Huntley really has a bad name among many students for underpaying when buying back textbooks, as well as having long lines. However it is convenient.

Don't Buy Books To Begin With - This is my preferred method. You can get books from the library, or through LINK+. If neither of those resources has your book, ask your professor to place a copy of the textbook on reserve at the library at the beginning of the semester. Or, anticipate your own future laziness and don't bother doing any of that.
