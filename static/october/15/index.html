Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality - Probably one of the top 2 books I've read this year. It's the story as if HP grew up as a rational scientist. "You turned into a cat! A SMALL cat! You violated Conservation of Energy! That's not just an arbitrary rule, it's implied by the form of the quantum Hamiltonian! Rejecting it destroys unitarity and then you get FTL signaling! And cats are COMPLICATED! A human mind can't just visualize a whole cat's anatomy and, and all the cat biochemistry, and what about the neurology? How can you go on thinking using a cat-sized brain?" If you don't like it by chapter 10, stop reading.
